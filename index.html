Welcome to the extraordinary world of paper flower design!

Greetings! I'm so glad you could stop by. You can call me Aunt Anne. My neice's wedding started it all off. After researching inexpensive decorations for an outdoor fall wedding, I got hooked on paper flower design. I started off with simple, coffee-filter designs, but my imagination got the best of me. I began to experiment with materials, textures, and color applications.

All designs are my original work. Please feel free to look around my site and drop me a line if you have any questions or comments. I'd love to hear from you.

Check out my Gallery for ideas and patterns. Be sure to visit the Resources page to see where I get supplies!
